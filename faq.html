Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Her Hub different from other websites?

+ -

Here are some key features that make us stand out: 1.Women-Centric Focus: HerHub is specifically designed to empower and uplift women in the freelancing and skill learning space. Our platform provides a safe and inclusive environment for women to explore, showcase, and enhance their talents without any gender bias.

2.Diverse Skill Categories: We offer a wide array of skill categories, ensuring that women from all walks of life can find opportunities that match their expertise. From programming and graphic design to writing and marketing, we cover an extensive range of skills.

3.Exclusive Community: By catering exclusively to women, HerHub fosters a strong sense of community and collaboration. Women can connect, network, and support each other, creating a supportive ecosystem that encourages personal and professional growth.

4.Mentorship and Guidance:HerHub understands the value of mentorship. We facilitate mentor-mentee connections, allowing experienced professionals to guide and inspire aspiring talents.

Can men join Her Hub?

+ -

Sure you can! How can we be true champions of diversity if we didn’t allow that? However, we are a jobs platform that champions women and marginalized genders so you may find that our content and coaching speaks to the challenges we face specifically as women. Most, if not all of the jobs listed on our platform will no doubt be listed in many other places by employers, so you won’t miss the opportunity to apply. We aren’t about taking anything away from men, we are about creating an equal playing field for women.

Is HerHub free to join for freelancers and learners?

+ -

Yes, HerHub is free to join for both freelancers and learners. Likewise, learners can also join HerHub for free and browse the various skill learning pathways available on the platform. They can explore the courses and resources offered in different categories and decide which skills they want to learn and develop.
